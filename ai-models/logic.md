The AI module in FedCollect AI assists in ethical and efficient debt collection by supporting prioritization, agent assignment, and compliance monitoring.

## Input Parameters
- Debt amount
- Overdue duration
- Customer repayment history
- Agent recovery success rate
- Complaint frequency and severity

## Processing Logic
- Rule-based scoring combined with lightweight machine learning
- Weighted priority calculation for each case
- Pattern detection for repeated complaints or policy violations

## Outputs
- Case priority score
- Recommended agent for assignment
- Trust score for agencies
- Compliance risk flags for internal review

## Ethical Considerations
- Prevents over-collection beyond credit limits
- Flags aggressive or repeated contact patterns
- Ensures transparency and accountability in decision-making
